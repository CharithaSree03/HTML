Social Media and Their Applications

Reading Platforms

  • YouTube-->YouTube is a global video-sharing platform where people upload, watch, and share videos. It offers content from music and tutorials to entertainment and news. YouTube
  • Quora-->Quora is a question-and-answer platform where people share knowledge and experiences. It helps users learn from others’ insights across different topics. Quora
  • Reddit-->Reddit is a network of communities where people discuss interests and share content. It has forums called "subreddits" for almost every topic. Reddit
  • Udemy-->Udemy is an online learning platform with thousands of video courses. It covers topics from programming to cooking, taught by experts. Udemy
  • Medium-->Medium is a platform for reading and writing articles on various topics. It connects writers and readers who share similar interests. Medium

Writing Platforms

  • Medium-->Medium allows users to publish their own articles and stories. Writers can share opinions, experiences, and creative work. Medium
  • Quora-->Quora lets people answer questions and give advice. It’s a space for experts and learners to connect. Quora
  • LinkedIn-->LinkedIn is a professional networking site for jobs, careers, and business connections. It also allows sharing professional content and articles. LinkedIn
  • Twitter-->Twitter is a microblogging platform where users post short messages called tweets. It’s widely used for news, trends, and discussions. Twitter
  • Facebook-->Facebook connects people with friends, family, and communities. Users can share updates, photos, and join groups. Facebook

Watching Platforms

  • YouTube-->YouTube offers videos ranging from entertainment to education. Users can subscribe to channels and get updates. YouTube
  • Instagram-->Instagram is a visual platform for sharing photos, videos, and reels. It’s popular for trends, influencers, and stories. Instagram
  • Netflix-->Netflix is a streaming service for movies, series, and documentaries. It offers both original and licensed content. Netflix
  • Udemy-->Udemy lets users watch educational videos on countless topics. Courses are available for self-paced learning. Udemy
  • JioHotstar-->JioHotstar streams live sports, TV shows, and movies. It includes both free and premium content. JioHotstar
