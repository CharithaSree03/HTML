Movie Review

Zootopia:

Rating: 9/10

Main Actors: Ginnifer Goodwin, Jason Bateman, Idris Elba

Highlights:

- Creative and colorful animation
- Engaging and funny storyline
- Positive social message about equality and understanding

Drawbacks:

- Some jokes may be missed by younger viewers
- Slightly predictable ending

Cast:

  1. Ginnifer Goodwin (Judy Hopps)
  2. Jason Bateman (Nick Wilde)
  3. Idris Elba (Chief Bogo)
  4. Jenny Slate (Bellwether)
